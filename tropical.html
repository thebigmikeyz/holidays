Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its white-sand beaches, clear blue waters, and luxury resorts. Activities include snorkeling, diving, and sunset cruises.

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is famous for its beaches, temples, and vibrant culture. Visitors can enjoy surfing, exploring rice terraces, and visiting the Ubud Monkey Forest.

Fiji

Fiji is known for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear lagoons, and coral reefs. Activities include snorkeling, diving, and enjoying the laid-back island life.
